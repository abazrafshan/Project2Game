1. This turn-based game starts with 2 players
  2. Each player rolls two dice to move along game board
  3. Each property on board has price, payout, and owned stat
  4. When players land on unowned property, they have option to buy the property
  5. Owned properties provide payouts to owners each time players pass “Go"
  6. Players pickpocket 10% of their opponent's wealth if they land on a space occupied by the opponent
  7. At the end of the game, program calculates each players’ net worth
  8. Player with highest net worth wins the game
